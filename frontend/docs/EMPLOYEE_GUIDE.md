# Employee User Guide

Everything you can do in Virata HR as an Employee.

## Signing in

Go to your company's Virata HR URL, enter your email and password. Forgot your password? Contact HR — there's no self-service reset in-app yet.

Press **Ctrl+K** (**⌘K** on Mac) anywhere to quickly jump to a page or check in/out without clicking through the sidebar.

## Your Dashboard

Your home screen: today's check-in status, your leave balance, your latest payslip amount, a calendar with your upcoming holiday, and a feed of your recent activity (check-ins, leave decisions, expense decisions).

## Attendance

**Check in** when you arrive and **check out** when you leave, from the Attendance page. This uses your device's location to confirm you're at a registered office site — if location access is blocked or you're outside every registered site, check-in may be refused or flagged, depending on your company's settings. If your device's location isn't available at all, you'll see a clear message rather than a silent failure.

Missed a check-in/out, or need to explain an anomaly? Use **Report a correction** on the same page — it goes to your manager/HR as a note.

## Leave

**Apply for leave**: pick a type, date range (or half-day), and a reason. Your current balance per leave type is shown on this page, and your request history below it. You'll get a notification when it's approved or rejected. You can cancel a request yourself as long as it hasn't been decided on yet.

## Payroll

View and print your own payslips from the Payroll page — a breakdown of earnings and deductions for each processed run, plus your net pay. You cannot edit your own salary or bank details here; that's set up by HR/Finance.

## Performance

During an open review cycle, you'll see your goals (if your manager has set any) with a progress field you can update yourself, and a **Submit your self review** form (a 1-5 rating plus strengths/areas to improve) once your manager opens that cycle. Your manager's review of you appears alongside your own once they've submitted it.

## Expenses

**Submit a claim**: category, amount, date, an optional description, and optionally one or more receipt links (paste a URL to an already-uploaded image or PDF of the receipt — there's no direct file upload here). You'll be notified once it's approved/rejected, and again once it's marked reimbursed.

## Calendar

One month view combining your attendance, your leave (color-coded by status), and company holidays. Click any day to see that day's details in the side panel.

## Notifications

The bell icon (top right) shows your most recent notifications with an unread count; click through to the full Notification Center to filter by channel or mark everything read at once.

## Things you won't see

Employees, Recruitment, Payroll administration, Reports, and Administration are HR/Admin/Manager/Finance-only — if a link isn't in your sidebar, it's genuinely not part of your role, not a bug to report.
